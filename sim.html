Hyogi Sim

Hyogi joined the Technology Integration group in 2013 as an intern. He is pursuing his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at Virginia Tech. His primary research interest is focused on deploying non-volatile memory devices to the distributed computing environments. He is also interested in general topics in file and storage systems.

He earned his M.S. degree in Computer Engineering in 2008 and B.S. in Civil Engineering in 2005, both from Hanyang University in South Korea.
