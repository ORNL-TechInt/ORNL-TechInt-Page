Doug Fuller

Doug joined the Technology Integration group in 2009 from Arizona State University, where he architected and led the implementation of high performance Linux Infiniband clusters based on Lustre, MPI, and OpenMP.

Doug received his B.Sc. from Iowa State University in 2002 and his M.Sc. in Computer Science from Iowa State University in 2005.

In addition to other projects, Doug led the effort to stand up the NOAA system Gaea.
