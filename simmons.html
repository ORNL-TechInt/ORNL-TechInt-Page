James Simmons

James joined ORNL in 2008 to work on the Lustre file system as a developer. four official releases, one in the works scheduled for April 30, 2013. State University of New York at Buffalo in 2000 with a B.Sc. in Physics. Interested in semiconductor technology. Got his start programming building experimental equipment in the physics lab. The only way to control the home built devices they built was by using open source drivers from Linux. He worked on a lot of cryogenic pumps. Has worked in the software industry for ten years on embedded devices and cellphone prototypes. He says he'll program anything as long as it's a Linux environment.
