Brian Smith

Brian joined Technology Integration in 2012.

Since joining TechInt, Brian has developed ParCAT, a parallel data analysis tool as part of the UV/CDAT project.

Prior to joining ORNL, Brian worked at IBM in Rochester MN. He was the team leader for the BlueGene/Q supercomputer messaging stack, a primary developer for the BlueGene L, P, and Q MPI implementations, and an IBM Master Inventor with over 100 filed patents.

Brian received both a B.S. in Computer Engineering and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering in 2000 and an M.S. in Computer Engineering in 2004 all from Iowa State University.
