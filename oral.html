Sarp Oral

Dr. Sarp Oral is currently a Research Development Associate at the National Center for Computational Sciences of Oak Ridge National Lab, where he is a member of the NCCS Technology Integration Group. Dr. Sarp Oral holds a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from University of Florida and an M.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering from Cukurova University, Turkey. During his Ph.D. studies Dr. Oral has served as the High Performance Networking Group Leader and also as the Associate Lab Director at the High-Performance Computing Simulation Lab, University of Florida under Dr. Alan D. George, his Ph.D. advisor. Also prior to joining to NCCS, Dr. Oral worked as an IT Systems Analyist with NCI Inc. as a sub-contractor to the Oak Ridge National Lab.
