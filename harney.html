John Harney

John received his Ph.D. from the University of Georgia in 2010. His dissertation was on research into novel strategies for optimizing Web queries.

As he was finishing his Ph.D., John also completed an internship in Technology Integration before joining the group as a full staff member in 2010.

John's role in Technology Integration has been to write and maintain the Web Front End for the Earth System Grid Federation, supporting the dissemination and sharing of climate data among climate scientists all over the world.
