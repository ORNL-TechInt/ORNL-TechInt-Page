Technology Integration members develop software in support of scientific data curation and dissemination through venues like ESGF, Mercury, CADES, etc.

Technology Integration efforts focus on data publication (EZ-Pub, search and discovery (ESGF Portal), retrieval (including from deep data archives like HPSS), and analysis (PARCAT, EDEN).

Contributors: John Harney, Ross Miller, Brian Smith, Tom Barron
