Chao Wang

Chao is a Research Associate of the Technology Integration (TechInt) Group in the National Center for Computational Sciences (NCCS) at Oak Ridge National Laboratory (he is currently employed through the University of Tennessee, Knoxville). His research topics include Parallel I/O, Storage Systems and Multicore Architectures; Distributed Computing/File System/Data Management; and HPC Resilience/Fault Tolerance.

He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from North Carolina State University. His doctoral work focused on MPI-level software redundancy for HPC applications; HPC resiliency system software for monitoring, fault prediction and proactive fault tolerance; and fault tolerance support for MPI: scalable membership, job pause, and process migration.

Previously, he received a M.Sc. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in China in 2001. He obtained a bachelors degree in Computer Science from Fudan University in China in 1998.
