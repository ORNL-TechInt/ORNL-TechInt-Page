Tom Barron

Tom is a Unix Systems Programmer. Since coming to ORNL in 2000, Tom has administered systems, written software on the HPSS project, and most recently worked on data publication in the Earth System Grid project. He received a B.S. in Computer Science from Vanderbilt University in 1982 and an M.S. in Software Engineering from University of Maryland in 2007. For HPSS, he has contributed to the Storage System Manager subsystem and currently owns and manages the Logging subsystem. In support of scientific data publication in ESG and other venues, he is currently working on a data publication tool called EZ-Pub.
