David Dillow

Dave Dillow is an Advanced Systems Ar- chitect and Developer at the National Center for Computational Sciences located at Oak Ridge National Laboratory. He joined NCCS in 2007 to deploy the first large-scale, routed Lustre file system, and he currently co-chairs Open Scal- able File Systems Technology Working Group. His research combines his interest in oper- ating systems, parallel computing, and high- performance networks and storage to design systems for future supercomputers
